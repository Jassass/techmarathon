Event Description

AppCombat is an Android Programming event in which enthusiastic App Developers come together over a single platform to have a friendly battle over Applications. 
Your age never defines you but your skills do, no matter either you are a male or a female, either you are a beginner or an expert. 
We welcome you all to our AppCombat and join together to boost and showcase your skills.

Prelims

 

  • The teams would be given an MCQ paper containing 30 questions.
  • The teams will be allotted 30 minutes to answer the paper.
  • The results will be announced and teams will be shortlisted for the next round.

 

Mains

 

  • In the final round each team will be Theme related question and they are required to strictly stick to the theme.
  • The Application will be judged on UI, functionality, speed, stability and performance.
  • Decision of judges will be final and abiding by all.

 

Rules

  • Team size must be of 2 students.
