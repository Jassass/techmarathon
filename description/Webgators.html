Prelims

 

  • The teams would be given an MCQ paper containing 25 questions.
  • The teams will be allotted 30 minutes to answer the paper.
  • The questions will be based on web programming languages such as HTML, CSS, php, javascript, jquery.
  • The shortlisted teams will be intimated at the earliest for the next round (Mains).

 

Mains

 

  • In this round, participants are required to make a website on a theme which will be provided to them.
  • The participants are allowed to use following web languages- HTML, CSS, PHP, JAVASCRIPT, JQUERY.
  • Time limit for this round is 120 minutes.
  • Website will be judged on the basis of :
    • Functionality
    • Interactive/Responsive design
    • Validation
    • Security
  • Decision of judges will be final and abiding by all.

 

Rules

  • The participants can register either individually or in a team of 2 members.
  • They all are provided with :
    • MYSQL
    • APACHE with PHP
    • TEXT EDITOR(sublimetext)
    • INTERNET connection
    • LINUX system(Ubuntu)
    • Simple Photo Editor(gimp)
    • Guidelines will be given about website appearance but NO images will be provided.
