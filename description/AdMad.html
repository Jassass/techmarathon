Event Description

Ad Mad, the Advertisement Selling Competition, to bring out that creative zing in you. 
Pull out all crazy ideas from your mind and mix them with ad making skills to market your concept.
Create jaw dropping moments for the target customers with your simple out of the box ideas.
Make it Hilarious!, Make it Sensitive!, Make it Comely or Prolific!, its completely up to YOU.
Ready to “Taste the thunder”?

 

Prelims

 

  • Topics will be provided to each team.
  • 20 minutes will be given to prepare the content.
  • Participants have to present an advertisement on the given topic/product.
  • Teams are allowed to bring props if in case, they prefer using them.

 

Mains

 

  • All the details for this round will be provided on the day of the event.

 

Rules

  • Team size must be of 3 or 4 students.
  • Teams should prepare the content of the ad in either English or Hindi and can mould their slogans in any language of their choice.
  • Caution should be taken to refrain from displaying obscenity, violence, prejudice, defamation etc. in advertisement.
  • Ads should not exceed 4 minutes, failing which negative marking will be done.
  • Participants will be judged on the basis of advertisement’s content- how humorous it is, expressions, presenter’s fluency, team coordination and presentation.
