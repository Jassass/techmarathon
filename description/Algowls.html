Event Description

Prelims

 

  • The teams would be given an MCQ paper containing 30 questions.
  • Questions are divided into three sections -
    • 15 questions of 1 marks with no negative marks.
    • 10 questions of 2 marks with 0.5 negative marks.
    • 5 questions of 3 marks with 1 negative marks.
  • The MCQ paper will be based on algorithms and its analysis.
  • The teams will be allotted 30 minutes to answer the paper.
  • The results will be announced and teams will be shortlisted for the next round (Mains).
  • Qualified teams will be provided with a story to be solved overnight
  • Incase of tie team who have more 3 marks questions correct will be given precedence. If they are same then the one who have more 2 marks questions correct. At last if all things are same, coin will be tossed to decide the winner.

 

Mains

 

  • A story will be given to teams via mail/portal after prelims.
  • Story will move around a set of problems, grouped in parts.
  • Problems lying in story may or may not be well known algorithms.
  • Teams are expected to present the precise solution to the given problems using PowerPoint presentations/white board and marker. Time Limit for presenting the solution is 15 min per team.
  • Teams are expected to answer all cross questions asked by judges and audiences.
  • Winners will be declared on the basis of feasibility, optimality, running time complexity and space complexity of the solutions. Alternate solutions, if provided might be considered for bonus points.
  • Decision of judges will be final and abiding by all.
  • In case of tie, the team who scored more in the prelims will be the winner.

 

Rules

  • It is a team based event Each team can have a maximum of 2 members.
  • Use of internet, pen-drives and any sort of helping material is strictly PROHIBITED.
  • Teams are not allowed to communicate with the other teams once the test starts
  • The decision of the organizers shall be final and binding. Organizers have the right to disqualify any team if they break any rule.
