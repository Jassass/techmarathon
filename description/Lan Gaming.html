Event Description

There will be 4 Games in Lan gaming - Counter Strike 1.6 , Need for Speed: Most Wanted, and FIFA and Mini Militia.

 

CS 1.6

 

  • A team should consist of 5 players only. If there are only 4 players present in a team, a stand in can be offered in place of 5th player. Teams with less than 4 players are not allowed to participate.
  • Registration Fees : Rs.100/- (Per Team).
  • At most 16 teams are allowed to participate.
  • Only 4 maps are chosen for both qualifiers and playoffs. These are:
    • -de_dust2
    • -de_inferno
    • -de_nuke
    • -de_train
  • Qualifiers will be of 16 rounds. After 8 rounds, teams switch sides. First team to score 9 points win the qualifier and advances to quarter finals. Losing team is eliminated.
  • In case of draw, there will be 6 rounds of overtime with starting cash of $10000. After 3 rounds, teams switch sides. Further draws will lead to more overtime.
  • Playoffs will be of 30 rounds. After 15 rounds, teams switch sides. First team to score 16 points advances to next round.
  • Each round time will be of 1 minute and 45 seconds, with extra 15 seconds of freeze time.
  • Quarter finals will be of 1 map only.
  • Semi-finals and finals will be of best of 3 maps. Either teams will pick a map. 3rd map will be decided on the spot.
  • All weapons with the exception of CT shield are allowed.
  • Teams can pick which side they want to play first. In case of dispute, sides will be selected by either coin toss or a knife round.
  • Teams will be given 3 minutes of warm up time where they can configure settings to suit their needs (e.g. controls, sensitivity etc.). No changes are allowed after the match has begun.
  • Silent bomb planting, boost bomb planting, flash bang bug are considered violations and will lead to disqualification.
    • Using pause is forbidden without the authorization of a tournament official (it can be used during the freeze time or the end of the round).
    • Any form of script is forbidden unless verified by official.
    • Using bugs which change the game principle (i.e. spawn bugs) is illegal.
    • Moving through walls, floors and roofs is strictly forbidden. This also includes skywalking. “silent bombs” (i.e. the planting of a bomb which doesn’t make a sound ) are illegal.
    • Planting bombs so that they cannot be defused is illegal. This does not include plants where multiple players are needed to defuse.
    • Defusing bomb through walls or roofs is strictly illegal.
    • Flashbugs are forbidden.
  • Players may not bring, install or use any external software or hardware that inter-acts in-game, any form of modification that would alter the game or allow a player to execute automatically more than one in- game action, or any configuration file, unless expressly authorized by a tournament official.
  • However, the players are allowed to bring their gears like mouse, headphones, keyboards etc. 
    Use of Rinput, mousefix and personal cfg is permitted once authorized by the tournament officials.

 

Need for Speed: Most Wanted

 

  • Registration Fees: Rs.50/-(per individual).
  • Competition Method: 4 players
  • Game Type: Circuit.
  • At the end of each match, players must maintain the final screens and receive confirmation from a referee.
  • Winning a race: The winner of a race is the first player to complete the laps of the circuit course and cross the finish line.
  • Graphic settings such as “motion blur” can be set using external utilities.
  • Any banned car to be decided by the referee.
  • Car Settings
    • Visual customizing allowed.
    • Personal save files are NOT allowed.
    • Reverb Team provided save file must be used for car setting.
    • Performance matching will be on so that all cars perform the same. Performance upgrades are not allowed.
  • Game Settings
    • Courses: Diamond, Dunwich Bay, Heritage Heights, Bay Bridge, East Park
      Each Course may be modified by the Reverb Team. Players will be informed before the tournament of any such modifications
    • The Course selection may be changed prior to the tournament. Players will be informed of any such changes before the tournament.
  • Race Mode Options: Circuit and Sprint (Final Rounds)
    • Track Direction : Forward
    • LAPS : random
    • N20 : On
    • Collision Detection : Off
    • Performance Matching : On
    • Min Number of Players : 2

  • Players are responsible for ensuring that their controller configuration is correct at all times.
  • General game settings:

    Half Length 10 Minutes
    Difficulty Level World Class
    Referee Random
    Time of Day Day
    Weather Dry
    Game Speede Normal
    Ball Default
    Injuries Off
    Offsides ON
    Bookings ON
    Handball Off
    User & CPU Game Customisation All set to 50%
  • Restart in case of Disconnection: any connection loss between opponents due to system, network, PC, and/or power problems/issues.
  • In case of a Draw: Penalties until the Semi-finals. Semi-Finals and Finals will have Extra time + penalties.
  • Game pause: Only allowed when the ball is out of bounds. Player to be disqualified if done intentionally otherwise.
  • All matches must end in a victory for one player. In the event of a draw after ninety (90) minutes of play, the rule Extra time or Penalties will be observed.
  • No equipment will be provided.
  • Limited entries first come first basis
  • PC Platform
  • Rules and Numbers can be changed if required.
Restrictions
  • Team Restrictions
    • Players can use any club or national team.
    • Players cannot use any custom made teams (e.g. Classic XI).
    • Players can swap teams at any point during the tournament.
  • Formation Restrictions
    • Players can only use default formations.
    • Players can use custom formations.
    • Players can use custom tactics.
  • Players have four (4) minutes to prepare their tactics at the start of a match. Subsequent changes are time limited to thirty (30) seconds per change.
Etiquette and Forbidden Moves
  • Players must kick-off with a backward pass at the beginning of each half and after every goal.
  • Players are not allowed to score goals from within their own half.
  • Players wishing to make substitutions or alter free-kick options must wait until the ball is out of play before making any changes.
  • Players must not pause the game when the ball is in play.
  • Players must not keep the ball in defence by passing it around (i.e. time wasting).
  • Players are not permitted to take control of the goalkeeper in order to make the AI take control of the defence.
  • Utilisation of game play aspects widely regarded as providing an unfair advantage or bad sportsmanship will not be tolerated. Any player found to be using these or unsporting behaviour will be removed from the tournament.
