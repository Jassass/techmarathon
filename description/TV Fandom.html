Event Description

TVF is an event where your free time pays off, not only coding or reading is productive.
Our event is based on your favorite English series like: F.R.I.E.N.D.S., Breaking Bad, Sherlock, Prison Break, etc. 
Participate and you'll realize, all the time you "wasted" binge watching those series wasn't actually wasted.
That "wastage of time" is here for you with a reward.

PILOT

 

  • It will be a written objective (and one word filling) type round with each team getting 30 questions to answer.
  • 10 marks for each correct answer, negative marking of 5 for each wrong answer.
  • The teams will be allotted 20 minutes to answer the paper.
  • The results will be announced and teams will be shortlisted for the next round.

 

The loser-champion segregation

 

  • This round contains two portions.
  • First, a rapid fire where each team is required to answer as many questions as possible in 60 seconds.
  • Second, a buzzer round where fastest finger first wins.

 

The end game vortex

 

  • PPT based round for which details will be provided on the spot.

 

Rules

  • Team size must be of 2 students.
