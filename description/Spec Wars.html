Event Description

If Exploring is your passion, If Gadgets are your theme, If Technology is your interest, Then you have come to the right place, TechMarathon’17 presents Specwars. Specwars is the technology and specifications quiz. 
The questions will be mostly related to tech world.

 

Prelims

 

  • The teams would be given an MCQ paper containing 20 questions.
  • The teams will be allotted 20 minutes to answer the paper.

 

Mains

 

  • Round 2 will comprise of various rounds which will be based on conventional quizzing methods like visual round, identification round rapid fire etc.

 

Rules

  • Participants will participate in a team of 2 or as lone wolf.
