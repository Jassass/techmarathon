Event Description

Pigeons were intercepted, Codes were broken, even Enigma was cracked. 
There is a way out of every box, a solution to every puzzle. It’s just a matter of finding it.
So warm up the Turing in you and head to the Bletchley Park of Delhi University – where waiting for you is an event that will force your brain to cycle hard and think out of the box. 
Sanganika – The Computer Science Department, DDUC welcomes you to the problem solving event, Conundrum at Tech Marathon’17

 

Prelims

 

  • Each team will be given 30 questions (one word or MCQs) to be solved within 30 minutes.
  • No Negative Marking.
  • Top 10 teams will be selected for next round.

 

Mains

 

  • Qualified teams will be provided with a set of problems (long story type questions) to be solved within an hour.
  • Internet Access is allowed.
  • Teams are expected to present the solution to the given problems.
  • Teams are also expected to answer the cross questions asked by judges.
  • Decision of judges will be final and abiding by all.

 

Rules

  • A Team of one or two is allowed.
