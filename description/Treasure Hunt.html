Event Description

'Your heart is where your treasure is, and you must find your treasure in order to make sense of everything.’

 

First Round (Prelims)

 

  • The teams would be given an MCQ paper based on General Aptitude and Current Affairs.
  • The teams will be allotted 20 minutes to answer the 30 questions.

 

Second Round ( Knock Out Round )

 

  • In this round, each team will be provided a list of 12-15 riddles which they have to solve and collect maximum numbers of items to win the round.
  • But there is a twist in this round which will be disclosed just before the round on the event day. Basically, this round tests their team spirit.
  • The teams will be given 90 minutes to complete this round.
  • Winning Teams will proceed to next round.

 

Third Round ( Finals )

 

  • In this round, each team will be provided a unique color code.
  • A computer system will also be allotting to each team to get the next clue; first clue will be provided by the organizing committee.
  • Each clue will be linked to some location. On that location, one of our organizing member will be there whom they have to search for, to get a password puzzle to unlock the system for the next clue.
  • Lots of twists and turns are waiting in this round too for our finalists, that will be disclosed on the same day.
  • This round includes a total of 6 main clues with 5 password puzzles.
  • The team that finishes all the clues first will be victorious.
  • The decisions made by the event organizers will be final and binding. 
    Any argument and/or nuisance caused to the organizers will not be tolerated and will lead to DISQUALIFICATION.

 

Rules

  • A Team can have 2 or 3 members.
  • Number of Teams getting eliminated after each round will be proporational to No of participating teams.
  • The Winning Team will take the Prize money home.
