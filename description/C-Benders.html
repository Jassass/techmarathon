Event Description

Welcome to the dark side! 
Do you believe your coding skills to be swift and merciless? 
Are you all for efficiency and quickest execution time possible?
Then, come forth to prove your resolve and unleash the force within you!

 

Prelims

 

  • The teams would be given an MCQ paper containing 30 questions.
  • 30% of total teams will be shortlisted for the next round.

 

Mains

 

  • For this round, the programming problems will be made available to the participants on February 17th at 8:00 P.M. and they will have to submit their code on February 18th by 6:00 A.M.
  • Any submission thereafter will not be accepted.
  • The team which will submit the maximum number of correct codes first, will be the winner.
  • In case of tie, Team who submitted answers early will be the winner.
  • The result will be declared at 9:30 A.M. on February 18th itself.
  • Decision of judges will be final and abiding by all.

 

Rules

  • Team size must be of 2 students.
