Event Description

Round 1

 

  • Round 1 is Object Perception Round.
  • An object will be assigned to each team (it could be anything like a piece of paper, laptop, book, desk, note or even any spot on the wall).
  • All the teams will be given 30 minutes after assigning of topic. In those 45 minutes teams will analyze the object and make a story. 
    The story must revolve around the assigned object .
  • After completing the story, teams will make the screenplay on which the final movie will be made.
  • After 30 minutes, all the teams will be given 5 minutes to explain the screenplay to the judges (any of the team member can explain the screenplay).
  • Judging will depend on the creativity of the team.

 

Round 2

 

  • This is going to be the final round.
  • The finalists will be given 2 hours to make a movie using their smart phones or camera.
  • The length of movie should be 5 to 8 minutes.
  • You must have any required software in your phone itself.
  • The language can be Hindi or English or it can be a musical movie.
  • You can’t use any outsider to act in your movie. Only your team members can act in that movie.
  • Winners will be decided on the basis of creativity, acting, story and overall impact of movie.
  • Decision of judges will be final and abiding by all.

 

Rules

  • A Team must have a minimum of 3 members or atmost 5 members.
