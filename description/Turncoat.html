Event Description

As the name suggests,Turn coat brings forward debating in an all new avatar where the students will have to debate against themselves and argue for both the sides of the coin. This all new style of debating will push you to contradict yourself, and that too convincingly ! 
Think you can change colours faster than a chameleon? Come over and try your hand at Turncoat

 

  • Only individual participations are allowed. No team participation.
  • Every participant will be given a topic on the spot on which he/she has to speak for 3-4 minutes.
  • Every participant will be given 5 minutes to prepare.
  • Languages allowed : English and Hindi
  • Last and the most important rule is everytime the buzzer is pressed the participant has to switch the motion i.e. If he is speaking in favor before the buzzer was pressed then he has to speak against that topic and vice-versa.
  • Winner will be decided on the basis of what he speaks, how he speaks and most important how he switches from favor to against and vice-versa.
  • Decision of judges will be final and abiding by all.
