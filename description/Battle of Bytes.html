Event Description

The real test that challenges the ability of a programmer is not just writing pages of code.
Maraud the bugs and sail your way through the gruelling event.

 

Rules

  • Languages to be used in the questions: Java and C++.
  • Team size must be of 2 students.
  • Use of internet, pen-drives and any sort of helping material is prohibited.
  • Teams are not allowed to communicate with the other teams once the test starts.
  • Organizers have the right to disqualify any team if they break any rule.

Prelims ( Round 1 )

 

  • The teams would be given a paper containing 30 questions of type MCQ, one word, one line.
  • It will contain 2 sections of equal weightage. Section 1 will be java based and section 2 will be C++ based.
  • The teams will be allotted 45 minutes to answer the paper.

 

Round 2

 

  • This will be a De-bugging round of 60 minutes.
  • It will have a total of 7 questions of both C++ and Java.
  • Programmers will have only 1 hour for these questions.
  • Marks will be rewarded for each corrected question at the end of event.
  • In case of tie, it will remain unresolved and all such teams will be qualified for next round.

 

Round 3

 

  • This will be a Coding round of 90 minutes.
  • It will have a total of 10 questions of both C++ and Java.
  • Marks will be rewarded for each corrected question at the end of event.
  • Decision of judges will be final and abiding by all.
  • In case of a tie, the rankings will be determined by the sum of the total time elapsed when you submitted the correct submission.
