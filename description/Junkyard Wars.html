Event Description

Tell me and I forget. Teach me and I remember. Involve me and I learn.

Now you are getting a chance to involve and explore the dominon of your dekstop's brain. If your hunger for knowledge taught you to do all of that , then come showcase those skill in junkyard war

 

Rules

  • Team size must be of 2 students.
  • Use of internet, pen-drives and any sort of helping material is prohibited.
  • All the teams must be present at the specified room/lab on the time.

Prelims - MCQ's(20 Ques - 30mins)

 

  • The teams would be given an MCQ paper containing 20 questions
  • The teams will be allotted 30 minutes to answer the paper.

 

Mains - Visual Round(10 Ques - 10 Rounds)

 

  • This will be a practical round. 2 Teams will be Qualified for this CPU assembly round.
  • The teams which assembles and boot the CPU first will be declared as the winner.
  • Damage or loss of any hardware part is responsibility of the participant, and he /she is liable to pay for it.
  • Decision of judges will be final and abiding by all.

 

Final Round

 

  • CPU Assembly Round
  • Participants will have to assemble a PC from scratch
  • Participants will be judged on the basis of speed and cable management.
  • Rest of the details of this round will be given on the spot.
