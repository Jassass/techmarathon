Event Description

"It's not about protocols, bytes or bits, it's about DDL and DML that tests your wits!" 
If digging out data is your hobby and SQL is your cool, 
then it's time to awake the Jedi inside you! 
Pull out your sabers and query your Shields.
This Tech Marathon, you are going to be "SQLized"

Prelims

 

  • The teams would be given an MCQ paper containing 30 questions.
  • The teams will be allotted 30 minutes to answer the paper.

 

Mains

 

  • This Round will have SQL problems based on multiple tables.
  • This is a Practical Round and you'll have to fetch the results as per the query.
  • Decision of judges will be final and abiding by all.
  • In case of tie, we may ask additional problems.

 

Rules

  • You can participate in a team of 2 or as a lone wolf.
  • RDBMS to be used is MySQL.
